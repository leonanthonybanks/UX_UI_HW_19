I learned UX/UI design through a Georgia Tech Bootcamp in Atlanta, Ga. I have always been a people person and have displayed a true passion for wanting to know what makes people tick? Why do we do the things we do?; a question I've always wondered.


I have worked on projects focused on user research, wireframing and UI design. When creating designs, I constantly refer back to my user research to make sure the intent of my design is in tact.
